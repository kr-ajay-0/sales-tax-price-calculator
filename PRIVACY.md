# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Sales Tax Price Calculator does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

## What data is used
- Tax settings you choose in the popup (country, region, or custom rate).

## Where data is stored
- Settings are stored locally in Chrome sync storage (`chrome.storage.sync`) so they can persist across your signed-in Chrome profile.

## What data is not collected
- No browsing history is stored by this extension.
- No selected page text is transmitted to any server.
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising scripts are used.

## Permissions
- `storage`: Used only to save and read your tax preferences.
- Site access (`<all_urls>` content script): Used only to detect selected text and show tax tooltip on webpages.
