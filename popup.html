Sales Tax Settings

How To Use

  1. Select your tax mode (Canada, US, or Custom).
  2. Click Save Settings.
  3. On any webpage, highlight a price like $19.99 or USD 49.
  4. You will see a tooltip with the after-tax amount.

Tip: phone numbers, order IDs, and list numbers are ignored on purpose.
